We are a group of inspired high schools trying to do our part to help the world for the better. Upon learning more about pressing global issues, we were motivated to start a school in order to help children in developping countries who are dealing with poverty and who are unable to access proper schools. To learn more about those who head this club, please click here.

 

By partnering with the Free The Children program, we became directly affiliated with the program and are constantly being supported by youth coordinators. The Free The Children program equipped us with helpful materials such as tool kits, manuals, and posters to help us inspire other youth around the world. To learn more about the Free The Children program, click here.

 

A subdivision of this program, Adopt A Village, encourages children all around the world to reach their full potential by giving them the rights to the things they deserve to have (namely education, water, and sustainable income). Click here to learn more about the program and see what we are working towards.
