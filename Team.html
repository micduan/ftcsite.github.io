Michael Duan

Michael Duan, a 16-year-old student, was inspired to start a Free The Children club ever since he partook in a We Day assembly in the 8th grade. Upon learning of the horrible conditions children in third-world countries had to go through, he was devastated and began to take steps to try and make a change in the world.

A passionate organizer and volunteer, Michael is extremely excited about this club and the goal of building a school.

Nyle Malik

As a dedicated volunteer and passionate student, Nyle Malik was always looking for ways to better himself, his community, and the world. Taking a lead in starting the Free The Children club was something that he felt very strongly about.

Upon starting this club, Nyle has become further involved in advocating for children's education and rights by taking an active role in clubs and committees such as Kiva and Science Expo.

Emmy Li

Upon taking a trip to China, Emmy was touched and saddened to see many women in her village who were forced to stay at home and work as a housewife due to their ineducation. Motivated to create a change, Emmy is working with the Free The Children club so that children across the world can have access to an education and build a better future for themselves.
